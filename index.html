How LightsCameraAction works

Step 1 · Create

Turn ideas into structured projects

Type your concept and let LCA (in the real product) generate loglines, breakdowns, characters, and a production-ready plan.

Step 2 · Cast & crew

Match humans to roles and jobs

AI suggests actors and crew based on look, skills, and style—humans always make the final casting call.

Step 3 · Shoot & deliver

AI production office in your browser

Auto-built schedules, call sheets, and continuity notes keep sets calm, respectful, and on time.
